## Ghi chu

- `{{MUC_DICH_THAM_DINH}}` dung cho noi dung ngan.
- `{{MUC_DICH_THAM_DINH_DAY_DU}}` dung cho cac mau can ghep san muc dich va nguon/doi tac.
- Mau ca nhan nam trong `samples/templates/individual/`.
- Mau to chuc nam trong `samples/templates/organization/`.
- Script chuan hoa mau: `tools/normalize_word_templates.py`.
